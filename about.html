History Mission Core Values Our Team Our business

Alibhai Motors - a name which is in Automobile industry since almost 50 years. Proudly served millions of customers and the journey is still continue with pride and honor, Alhamdulillah. Alibhai Motors started operations in 1970s. In few years after starting the operations, the customer satisfaction ratio got higher due to quality of service being provided.

It was 1998, when Alibhai Motors started business venture with Honda Atlas Cars Pakistan Limited (HACPL) and established a 3S (Sales, Services, and Spare parts) dealership by name "Honda Drive-In". In a very short time span, because of consistent efforts and remarkable quality of services, we turned out among the best dealerships of HACPL. We have been awarded as the Best 3S Dealership (Southern Region) in May 2017 at the Dealers Meeting held in Sydney, Australia.

By consistent hard work and dedication, Honda Drive-In has now the largest workshop in which contains a team of Engineers, Certified Technicians and Qualified Advisors working under an umbrella of incredibly professional management.
