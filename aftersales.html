Periodic Maintenance & Inspections

THOROUGLHY TAKING CARE OF YOUR CAR AFTER SALE

What is Periodic Maintenance & inspection?

Periodic Maintenance is a series of inspections, lubricants-replacement, parts adjustment services at specified time-intervals.

Under Periodic Maintenance and inspections, our experts do diagnosis, inspections, fluid replacements, and parts adjustments just to ensure you a trouble-free drive and reliability and healthy life of your Honda car!

Is Periodic Maintenance & Inspection necessary for your car?

Certainly Yes! Performing periodic maintenance & inspections are quite important for a smooth performance and healthy life of your vehicle.

How we do Periodic Maintenance & Inspections?

Timely Maintenance of your vehicle will help in resale and maintenance of your vehicle warranty. In periodic maintenance vital parts of your vehicle are inspected to prevent vehicle breakdown/failure. Some basic parts which we cover in periodic maintenance are:

  • Engine Components including
  • Drive belt
  • Cooling system
  • Pulleys
  • Suspension leakages
  • Under carriage nuts and bolt retrorque
  • Air filter
  • Intake and exhaust system
  • Engine Oil condition
  • Brake oil condition
  • Power Steering fluid
  • Transmission fluid
  • Spark plugs and many more

But, you warranty cannot be claimed if,

Warranty

We provide you a written guarantee of proper and smooth functioning of your Honda vehicle when you purchase it from our dealership: and in warranty tenure if your Honda car malfunction, we will repair or replace it free of cost.

But, you warranty cannot be claimed if,

  • Routine periodic maintenance of your Honda Car is not performed especially the first 1000 km, 5000 km or 10,000 km. For verification the warranty book needs to be stamped against each routine periodic maintenance performed.
  • Any alteration or modification in or to the car which results in damage to a certain part, for example: trackers/anti-theft devices; over-sized tires; CNG kit; and spoilers etc.
  • The car gets serviced at an unauthorized workshop (other than an Authorized Honda Dealerships).
  • The warranty booklet is lost or not properly maintained.
  • Any kind of Non-Genuine parts installed in the car.
  • Exploitation of car (for example, using car for racing, etc.) will terminate warranty.
  • Your vehicle is unregistered.
